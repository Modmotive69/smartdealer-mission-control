Our Story

Built by the People Who Invented Automotive eCommerce

In 1994, Scott Anderson built one of the world's first automotive parts eCommerce platforms. In 1998, Parts.com reached a $1B market cap on NASDAQ and became AutoNation's exclusive online fulfillment partner. He then built TradeMotion — the first SaaS platform for OEM parts & accessories — powering Volkswagen, Subaru, Kia, Nissan, and Chrysler's first Mopar DTC store. Today, SmartDealer Technologies is the holding company for InDesign Data LLC and PartSites LLC — representing 30 years of automotive e-commerce expertise.
