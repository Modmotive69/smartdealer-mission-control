What Is It

A National Parts Marketplace Built on PartSites Infrastructure

MARKETPLACE is a national OEM parts and accessories destination — a single storefront that aggregates inventory from participating dealers across the PartSites network and connects buyers to the right dealer, right part, right price.

For OEMs, it's a direct-to-consumer channel that doesn't require building new technology. For dealers, it's additional traffic and revenue from national buyers who wouldn't have found them otherwise.

Built on the same ENVY PRO infrastructure that has generated over $500 million in dealer parts sales — MARKETPLACE is the natural evolution of the PartSites network.

How It Works

The Parts Network Effect

1

Dealers Join PartSites

Dealers subscribe to ENVYPRO and activate their parts eStore. Their inventory becomes part of the network.

2

OEM Threshold Reached

Once 100+ dealers in an OEM network are live on PartSites, the OEM qualifies for a free MARKETPLACE storefront.

3

National Buyers Find Dealers

MARKETPLACE drives national organic traffic — buyers searching for OEM parts find the right dealer with inventory in stock.
